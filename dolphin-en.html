About Dolphin Beach

Dolphin Beach is a distinctive resort in Ain Sokhna featuring a hotel and coastal homes including villas. It enjoys a private sandy beach and a comprehensive mix of essential and leisure services by El Hegaz Development. Ain Sokhna itself lies on the Red Sea, close to Cairo, and hosts a variety of upscale resorts and hotels.

As one of the earliest destinations in the area, Dolphin Beach Resort is a fully serviced village by El Hegaz for Tourism Development. It comprises a hotel and 450 homes split into chalets and standalone villas. The resort is around 1.5 hours from Cairo on the Zaafarana Road and includes multiple swimming pools, a private beach and diverse entertainment.

Developer

El Hegaz for Tourism & Real Estate Development is the developer of Dolphin Beach. Founded in 1988, the Egyptian company delivers modern concepts and high-quality hospitality services, adheres to on-time handovers, invests in team development and continues to grow with a diverse portfolio across the Egyptian real-estate market.
