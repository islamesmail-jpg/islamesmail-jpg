About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano Ain Sokhna is a tranquil retreat away from the city bustle, yet close enough for quick trips. On the Red Sea shores, it blends nature’s charm with modern comfort, designed to provide serenity with all amenities and entertainment — plus unique natural hot springs.

Location

At the end of Ain Sokhna Road and start of Zaafarana Road — the closest Red Sea destination to Cairo, with excellent access to major highways.

Key Distances

  • Galala Road: 18 km
  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: 14 km
  • Cairo: 136 km
  • Katameya Road: 112 km
