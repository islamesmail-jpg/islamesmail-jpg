About Murano Wadi Degla

Murano in Ain Sokhna offers a peaceful escape on the Red Sea, combining natural beauty with modern comfort. Carefully planned to provide a relaxing environment with full amenities, and uniquely close to Cairo thanks to its strategic location at the end of Sokhna Road and start of Zafarana Road.

Prime Location

The project enjoys direct access to major roads and key destinations, ensuring easy commute from different parts of Egypt.

Nearby Roads & Landmarks

  • Ain Sokhna Train Station: 14 km
  • Cairo: 136 km
  • Kattameya Road: 112 km

Land Area & Planning

Murano spans a generous area with smart zoning between homes, greenery and lagoons, creating privacy and comfort with vibrant recreational facilities.
