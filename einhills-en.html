About Ein Hills

Ein Hills is El Shahawei Group’s debut in Ain Sokhna — set on the Galala plateau with panoramic Red Sea views, European-inspired architecture, wide landscapes and water features, and a fully serviced lifestyle with flexible payment plans.

Developer

El Shahawei Group (est. 1976) delivers residential & commercial projects and owns leading companies including Arab Cement and Al Sukhkhour for marble.

Signature Projects

  • Bali — North Coast
  • Movenpick El Sokhna Hotel

Location

On Galala plateau in the heart of Ain Sokhna — ~127 km from Cairo, 8 km to Galala City, around 60 minutes from New Cairo.

  • New Capital: ~47 km (~45 min)
  • Sokhna Gates: ~15 km
  • Galala City: 8 km
  • Porto Sokhna: ~15 min
  • New Cairo: ~100 km

Land Area

~50 feddans, ~800 m beachfront width and ~250 m depth, ~500 units with premium sea views; extensive green areas, water features, F&B, ~100 m² retail, crystal lagoons and ~4,200 m² waterfalls.

Design

Tiered terraces to secure sea views for all units; contemporary facades and weather-resistant materials; lush landscapes and multiple artificial lakes.
