About Ein Hills

Ein Hills is El Shahawi Group’s first launch in Ain Sokhna, set on an elevated hillside with sweeping Red Sea views. The resort blends vast greenery, water features and European-inspired contemporary design, delivering a fully serviced lifestyle with modern tech and abundant leisure — plus flexible payment plans.

Developer – El Shahawi Group

One of Egypt’s leading developers since 1976 with 30+ years of experience, delivering successful residential and commercial projects. The group also owns leading companies such as Arab Cement and Ready Rocks for marble.

Signature Projects

  • Bali – North Coast
  • Mövenpick El Sokhna Hotel

Location

On Galala Plateau in the heart of Ain Sokhna — 127 km from Cairo, 8 km from Galala City; around 60 minutes from Cairo.

  • New Administrative Capital: 47 km (~45 min)
  • Ain Sokhna Gates: ~15 km
  • Galala City: 8 km
  • Porto Sokhna: ~15 min
  • New Cairo: ~100 km

Site Area

Approx. 50 feddans with ~800 m beachfront and ~250 m depth, ~500 sea-view units. Extensive greenery, water features, retail & dining (~100 m²), crystal lagoons & waterfalls (~4,200 m²).

Design

World-class terraced masterplan ensuring sea views for most homes, modern façades and durable materials, with generous landscaping and multiple artificial lakes.
